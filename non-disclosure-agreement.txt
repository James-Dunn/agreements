Confidentiality Agreement

This website, http://www.companyslice.com,  discloses certain information about other companies that must be kept confidential. The following confidentiality agreement shall apply to information about all companies listed on this website. To ensure the protection of such information, it is agreed that:

1. The information to be disclosed can be described as and includes:
	a. The status of a company listed, such as being for sale, raising equity, issuing debt, or any other information related to the purpose of the listing
	b. The terms of the offering, such as the term and interest rate offered on debt, number of shares issued, classes of shares offered, or any other information about the offering.
	c.  Any trade secrets,  or descriptions of current or future business ideas
	d. Financial statements and any information related to the finances of the company
	e. Any non-public information. Any information on the website that is not viewable without logging in shall be assumed confidential

2. The Recipient agrees not to disclose the confidential information obtained from this website to anyone unless required to do so by law.

3. This Agreement states the entire agreement between the parties concerning the disclosure of Confidential Information. Any addition or modification to this Agreement must be agreed to by both parties.

4. If any of the provisions of this Agreement are found to be unenforceable, the remainder shall be enforced as fully as possible and the unenforceable provision(s) shall be deemed modified to the limited extent required to permit enforcement of the Agreement as a whole.
